Model Structure

The page summarizes the shipped Python model and archived input data.

Poisson scoring

Each team gets an expected goal rate from attack, defense, Elo difference, recent form, and rest days.

Knockout tie-break

Drawn knockout matches are resolved by a logistic Elo tie-break calibrated from historical shoot-outs.

Monte Carlo bracket

The remaining seven matches are simulated 100,000 times to estimate conditional champion probabilities.
